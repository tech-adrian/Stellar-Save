System States & Loaders
Standardized visual feedback for loading, empty, and error conditions. Ensuring users are never left wondering what’s happening with their property search or dashboard data.

01. Skeleton Loaders
Placeholders that simulate the layout of content before it’s fully loaded, reducing perceived wait time.

Property Card Placeholder: A large block layout simulating image and text areas.

Dashboard Stat Placeholder: Circular and rectangular shapes representing profile and numerical data.

02. Empty States
No Results Found
We couldn't find any properties matching your current filters. Try adjusting your search criteria or price range.

Action: [Clear All Filters]

03. Error States
ERROR 404: Something went wrong
Our system encountered an unexpected error while fetching the property details. This might be a temporary issue with our servers.

Actions: [Retry Now] [Go Back]

04. Offline State
Used when the browser detects a loss of internet connectivity.

Banner Notification: "You are currently offline. Some features may be unavailable."

Action: [Check Connection]

Modal/Overlay: Connection Lost

"Please check your internet settings. We'll reconnect automatically."

Status: "Attempting to reconnect..."
