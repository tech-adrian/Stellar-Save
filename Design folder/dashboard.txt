To create the design for Stellar Save, I focused on translating the complex mechanics of a decentralized ROSCA into a user-friendly mobile interface. Hereâ€™s a quick breakdown of the design process:

Information Hierarchy: I prioritized high-level financial health (Total Saved, Payout Dates) at the top of the dashboard to give users immediate peace of mind and clarity.
Visual Progress Tracking: For the Group Details and Dashboard, I used progress bars and "Round X of Y" indicators to make the rotational nature of the savings circles easy to understand at a glance.
Trust & Security: By using a clean, professional "Stellar Blue" color palette and a modern sans-serif font (Manrope), the design evokes the reliability expected of a financial application built on blockchain technology.
Onboarding Logic: I designed a dedicated empty state to provide a clear path for new users, ensuring the app feels welcoming and intuitive from the very first login.
Social Context: Since ROSCAs are inherently social, I included member status and recent activity feeds to keep users connected to their group's collective progress.
